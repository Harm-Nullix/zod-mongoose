# Online Playground

Our online playground is based on the Studio and allows you to experiment with `mongoose-zod` directly without installing anything.

## What can you do?

- **Experiment**: Define your own Zod schemas and see the results.
- **Learn**: Discover how different Zod validators are mapped to Mongoose options.
- **Shareable**: Easily share your schemas and configurations with others.

The playground is powered by Nuxt and provides a seamless experience for developers to explore the capabilities of `mongoose-zod`.
